I am writing to you as the registered owner of the property at [PROPERTY ADDRESS] (Lot [LOT] on Plan [PLAN]).

My company specialises in identifying land with residential subdivision potential in South East Queensland. We have been researching opportunities in the [SUBURB/REGION] area and believe your property may present an exciting opportunity.

Rather than a traditional purchase, we typically work with landowners through a Call Option Agreement — a simple arrangement where we secure the right to purchase your property at an agreed price within a set timeframe, while we complete the necessary council approvals and due diligence. You receive an upfront, non-refundable option fee, and there is no obligation on your part unless we proceed.

This approach means no disruption to your current use of the property while we work through the planning process. If the project doesn't proceed, you keep the option fee and retain full ownership.

Scoring Methodology

Dwelling Supply Gap (25pts) — Difference between 2046 target and current stock. Larger gap = more demand for new lots.
Expansion Ratio (20pts) — % of growth on greenfield vs infill. Higher expansion = more subdivision opportunity.
Population Growth Rate (15pts) — Projected growth rate to 2046. Faster growth = stronger demand.
Infrastructure Investment (15pts) — Proximity to RSI projects (rail, road, social). Infrastructure drives land value.
PDA/PFGA Catalyst (15pts) — Government-accelerated zones with streamlined approvals and trunk infrastructure funding.
Competitor Validation (10pts) — Presence of Stockland, Lendlease, Peet, AVID etc. validates demand. Sweet spot: some competitors but not saturated.
